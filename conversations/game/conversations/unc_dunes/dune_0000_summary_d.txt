Hero: So, Elaaden. Charming so far. What do we got... besides the sand?
SAM: The Remnant monolith suggests this was indeed supposed to be a golden world. It also suggests the presence of a vault.
SAM: Additionally, here is the large derelict Remnant ship that we saw as we landed.
Hero: Hopefully abandoned?
SAM: Scans indicate it is not. The site is active with scavengers and Remnant bots.
SAM: Here, scavengers have taken over an abandoned camp between the two.
SAM: In this direction, located in one of the planet's sinkholes, is New Tuchanka, the krogan colony.
Hero: Sinkholes. Sounds fun.
SAM: Not really. Elaaden is tidally locked to its sister moon and that gas giant above. This side of the moon never cools.
SAM: Getting trapped in a sinkhole without shelter would mean certain death, as one's blood would boil in 75 seconds.
Hero: And on that cheery note, let's head for the climate-controlled paradise of the Nomad.
