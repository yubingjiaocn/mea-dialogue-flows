aya_vista_angara_01: If the kett are turning us into them, then maybe my sister...
aya_vista_angara_02: No. Don't say it.
aya_vista_angara_01: But she could be out there.
aya_vista_angara_02: She wouldn't be herself. You can't think about it.
aya_vista_angara_02: Have you met their new ambassador? She's...
aya_vista_angara_01: Not one of us.
aya_vista_angara_02: Oh, Vestja. Don't be so quick to judge.
aya_vista_angara_02: I'm bringing her a paripo. As a welcome gift.
aya_vista_angara_01: I... I don't even know how to feel. We were... created?
aya_vista_angara_01: When we die... do we not give strength to our descendants? What do we become?
aya_vista_angara_02: Stop. It's not true. Just more kett lies. They want us to doubt.
aya_vista_angara_02: We can't let them destabilize us again.
