aya_memgard_visitor [OncePerGame]: This is my first visit to Aya. It's more beautiful than I ever imagined.
aya_memgard_horticulturist: Thank you. I work hard to keep it that way.
aya_memgard_visitor: Why do they call it the "Memorial Garden"?
aya_memgard_horticulturist: Many of these plants came from places we lost to the kett. It's all we have to remember them by.
aya_memgard_visitor: Does looking at them ever make you sad?
aya_memgard_horticulturist: All the time. Then I remember that if they survived, so can we.
