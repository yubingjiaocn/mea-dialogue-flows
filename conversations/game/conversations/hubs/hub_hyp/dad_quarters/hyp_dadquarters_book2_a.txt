rydfam_asari: When studying a dead race, the most obvious problem is the lack of an observable population.
rydfam_asari: This raises the question: When is a species truly extinct?
rydfam_asari: The Prothean civilization spanned the galaxy, and it took centuries to confirm that all members of the species really had died out.
rydfam_asari: While it's possible that living Protheans may be found in a hidden pocket of the galaxy one day, the chances are statistically trivial.
rydfam_asari: Once an extinction is confirmed, the study's nature moves from sociology to exo-archaeology.
